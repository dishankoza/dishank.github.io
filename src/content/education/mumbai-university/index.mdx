### SAE Aero Design Competition
**Team Vaayushastra**: Represented Fr. CRCE in the SAE Aero Design Competition.
* **Competition Overview**: The Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) conducts an annual aero design competition in the USA, featuring three classes: advanced, regular, and micro. Teams design, fabricate, and fly Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs).
* **Achievement**: Ranked **9th** globally and **2nd** in the Asia-Pacific region in the SAE Aero Design Competition 2020, held at Lakeland, Florida.
* **Personal Contribution**:
    1. Developed an algorithm to guide the aircraft to its destination.
    2. Created a system to send aircraft sensor data to the ground control station.
    3. Designed a system for the aircraft to drop food and secondary aircraft from the primary aircraft.
